This is an entry for @sableRaph's weekly creative coding challenge. The theme for this week was "hyperspace." I wanted to capture the feeling if looking down the length of a subway and seeing the world whoosh by you through the windows. Interestingly, p5 limits you to 5 lights at a time, so each subway car is rendered with only 4 lights (5 would be asymmetrical), and only with its own 4 lights, so there's a little lighting discontinuity between each car.
