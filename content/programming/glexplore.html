This is part of a longer-term project to abstract away the complicated handshakes you have to do to create OpenGL shaders and ferry information to, from, and between them. In order to get a better idea of what is involved in this project, we spent some time writing vanilla GLSL. Here's what we accomplished: - Multiple variable point lights - Point light shadows - Shadow blur - Phong shading - Smooth shading - Perlin noise - Movable camera
